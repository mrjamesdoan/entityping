Frequently asked questions

What is a business filing checker?

A business filing checker lets you look up recently registered businesses in a given state. When someone forms an LLC, corporation, or DBA, it gets filed with the Secretary of State. Our tool aggregates these filings so you can see new businesses as they register.

How often are business filings updated?

EntityPing pulls new filings every business day. Most filings appear in our system within 24 hours of being recorded by the state. The free checker shows a sample of recent filings; paid plans deliver the full daily feed by 7 AM ET.

Which states are covered?

The free filing checker covers Florida, Texas, Georgia, Colorado, New York, and California. EntityPing's full service covers all 50 states with daily filing data including owner names, addresses, phone numbers, and industry classification.

Can I get daily business filing data delivered to my inbox?

Yes. EntityPing delivers fresh business leads from state filings as a clean CSV email every morning. You can filter by state, industry, and entity type. Plans start at $39/month for 200 leads per day. You can also get a free sample of 25 leads to try it out.

What information is included in each filing?

State filings typically include the business name, entity type (LLC, Corporation, DBA), filing date, registered agent, and principal address. EntityPing enriches this data with owner phone numbers, email addresses, and industry classification so you can start outreach immediately.

Is this data free to use?

The filing checker tool is completely free. It shows a sample of recent filings in each state. For the full daily feed with contact information and enrichment, you can sign up for a free sample of 25 leads or choose a paid plan starting at $39/month.
