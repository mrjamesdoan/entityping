FAQ

Common questions

Where does the data come from?

Multiple public sources: state Secretary of State filing records, industry directories, and permit databases. We aggregate, classify, and quality-score everything into a single clean feed.

How fresh are the leads?

We pull new filings every day. Most leads are delivered within 24 hours of the business registering with the state. Growth and Pro plans get priority delivery by 7 AM ET.

Do you include phone numbers and emails?

It depends on the source. Industry directory leads typically include phone numbers. State filing leads include owner name, address, entity type, and industry. Email enrichment is available as an add-on for Growth and Pro plans.

Can I filter by industry or location?

Absolutely. Choose the industries that match your business, and you'll only receive leads in those categories. All plans include geographic filtering by city, county, or zip code.

What format do the leads come in?

Clean CSV files that open directly in Excel, Google Sheets, or any CRM that supports CSV import. No special software required. Zapier and CRM integrations are on our roadmap.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. No contracts, no cancellation fees. All plans come with a 7-day money-back guarantee. Cancel anytime and retain access through the end of your billing period.
