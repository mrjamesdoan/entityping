Frequently Asked Questions

How can construction companies use new business filing leads?
Every newly registered business is a potential construction client. Retail businesses need tenant improvements and buildouts. Restaurants need kitchen builds. Offices need renovations. By reaching out within days of filing, you catch them during the planning phase — before they have chosen a contractor.
Can I filter leads to specific business types that need construction?
Yes. EntityPing lets you filter by industry classification. Focus on restaurants, retail stores, medical offices, fitness studios, or any business type that typically requires commercial construction, tenant improvements, or specialized buildouts.
What information is included with each lead?
Every lead includes the business owner's full name, business name, registered address, phone number, entity type (LLC, Corp, etc.), filing date, and industry classification. Growth and Pro plans also include email enrichment so you can follow up via email campaigns.
How fresh are the leads?
Leads are delivered within 24 hours of the business filing with the state. This means you are reaching out during the earliest planning stages — when the business owner is actively looking for contractors, architects, and tradespeople to build their vision.
