Frequently Asked Questions

Why are new business filings valuable leads for marketing agencies?
Every new business needs a website, logo, social media presence, and marketing strategy. When you reach out within days of their filing, you catch them before they have hired an agency or freelancer. They are actively looking for help building their brand — making them highly receptive to outreach from marketing professionals.
Can I target specific types of businesses that spend on marketing?
Yes. EntityPing lets you filter by industry classification. Focus on business types that typically invest heavily in marketing — restaurants, e-commerce brands, professional services, health and wellness, and real estate companies. You can also filter by geography to stay within your service area.
How do I use these leads for agency outreach?
Most agencies import the daily CSV into their CRM and run automated email sequences or have their BDR make personal calls. The key advantage is timing — you are reaching out when the owner is still setting up their business and actively needs marketing help. Many agencies offer a free audit, website mockup, or brand consultation as their opening offer.
What conversion rates can marketing agencies expect?
Conversion rates vary by outreach method and offer, but agencies typically see 2-5% response rates on cold email sequences to new filings, compared to under 1% on aged lists. Phone outreach within 48 hours of filing typically yields even higher engagement, with many agencies reporting 10-15% contact rates and 3-5% close rates.
